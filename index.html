ITR-4 SUGAM — Presumptive Income Form
✅ Use ITR-4 if: Your business turnover is ≤ ₹2 Crore and you want to declare income at 8% of turnover (Section 44AD). Best for small shops, traders, and small service businesses.

Auto-fills: Name, business details, gross turnover, presumptive profit (8%), tax computation with new regime slabs, GST summary, and verification declaration.

ITR-3 — Business Income with Books of Accounts
✅ Use ITR-3 if: Your turnover exceeds ₹2 Crore, or you maintain full books of accounts and do NOT want to use the presumptive scheme.

Auto-fills: P&L Account, simplified Balance Sheet, business income schedule, tax computation, expense category breakdown, and verification.
